Structured S&C
Built for Athletes

Built to Perform is a structured, periodised S&C programme for athletes who are serious about building a performance base. You get a full programme built around the specific demands of your sport, updated to suit your current season phase.

Training is delivered entirely through the Fitr app — structured, progressive, and ready to follow from Day 1. This is for athletes who want a proven system without the need for personal one-to-one coaching support.

Every training block is designed around your sport's seasonal calendar — so you're in the right shape at the right time, every time it counts.

Who's it for?
  • — You just want a plan — clear sessions, clear progressions, no second-guessing
  • — You're consistent and self-sufficient in the gym
  • — You don't need ongoing support — just a proven structure that works
What's Included
  • — Programme built specifically around the demands of your sport
  • — Programme updated to suit the time of season & make sure you're at your best when it matters most
  • — All programming delivered through the app — accessible anytime, anywhere
  • — Access to the community — connect with other athletes on the same journey
  • — Programming that automatically adjusts to your current season phase — in-season, off-season, pre-season
